Aranaht: Where Two Worlds Merge



Petra Aranaht


Idra Aranaht

Aranaht is set in an ultra-futuristic universe spanning two visually stunning planets, Arikun and Zohrati. Each defined by towering megacities, advanced military technology, and vast open landscapes waiting to be explored. Designed as an open-world experience, the game allows players to traverse sprawling urban hubs, heavily fortified spaceports, and distant frontiers under alien skies illuminated by twin celestial bodies. From hovering warships to elite armored factions, every element of the world reflects a high-tech civilization on the edge of transformation, where exploration, choice, and consequence shape the player’s journey.


At the heart of Aranaht lies a growing conflict between Arikun and Zohrati, two rival planets locked in a tense and escalating war. Co-Leading each world are Petra and Idra, powerful Deputy Chiefs of State raised on opposite sides of the divide. Their realities are shattered when they uncover a hidden truth—they share the same surname, Aranaht, and are in fact sisters separated by a long-buried past. As political pressure mounts and loyalties are tested, both must grapple with whether to remain enemies or forge an uncertain alliance. Meanwhile, a mysterious third faction begins to emerge from the shadows, manipulating events from behind the scenes, while fragments of forbidden technology hint at a deeper origin linking both planets. One that could either unite them or bring about their total destruction.
